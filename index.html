About Me

Eric Jobin is a learner at the University of Toronto where he's studying Full Stack Web Development. Undertaking an experimental 12 week intense boot-camp style course, he is preparing for a career in the web development industry and building up a portfolio of work to show off to potential employers.

Eric Jobin lives in North York, in the Newtonbrook neighbourhood, where he resides in a small condo apartment in a very tall building, surrounded by other very tall buildings. The neighbourhood is known for its easy access to public transportation, and a number of Korean restaurants. Eric can find kimchi on sale at any time of day or night.

When Eric is not studying or working on web development projects, he can usually be found playing boardgames, drinking tea, or reading.
